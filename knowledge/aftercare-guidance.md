# Aftercare Guidance

## Scope
Use this only for general, low-risk aftercare guidance.

## Safe general guidance
- Keep answers general unless the exact aftercare instruction is clearly known.
- Encourage customers to follow the salon team's direct advice if they have just had a treatment.
- For pain, swelling, bleeding, strong irritation, or anything that looks medical, direct the customer to contact the salon first.

## Good examples
- Be gentle with fresh nails and avoid picking or forcing product off.
- If a customer asks about removing or fixing lifting, suggest contacting the salon rather than attempting a risky home fix.
- If they ask whether the salon can review a concern, invite them to upload a photo or contact the salon directly.

## Do not do
- Do not give medical advice.
- Do not promise that a home fix is safe.
- Do not invent aftercare steps for a specific treatment if they are not confirmed.
